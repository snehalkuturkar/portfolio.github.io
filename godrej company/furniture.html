To make homes truly intuitive, we spend time on the finer, unseen details. Our market-leading range of architectural fittings and fixtures help create seamless spaces: smart hinges that open and close doors intelligently; gliders that make cupboards and drawers silent. Our engineering expertise helps us create products that make home interiors more durable and secure.

We pay close attention to the hidden details because we believe they play an important role in contributing to the overall experience.We know that ergonomics promotes good health and our team of experts not only inform and guide design decisions but also perform customer audits and drive awareness on trends in posture and wellness as this enhances productivity. Our strong domain expertise helps us adapt traditional uses of space to create contemporary solutions for future- forward workspaces in offices or at home.We believe our designs should address real pain points. We know that urban homes are shrinking and real estate is expensive. Our innovative range of compact, multi-purpose furniture can easily be put away when not in use, making place for something else.

We converted a living room into a bedroom or a dining room and back into a living room, and optimised space constraints without having to compromise on anything. We believe our processes and our products should be safe and sustainable. Our manufacturing processes are Greenguard certified which means that our products are safe for you and for our planet. All our wood is sustainably sourced and free from harmful chemicals.We help our customers create spaces that reflect their individuality and lifestyle. Whatever your needs, drop in to see us or visit us online. We would be more than happy to help you bring your vision to life.
