When we decided to enter the Food & Agri-Business, we had one vision in mind. To bring India the best of food and take care of those who make it - our farmers and our animal friends. Since then, we have gone on to become the leading business providers from consumer foods to focused agro-based needs, with our expertise running across the entire food supply chain.

Our Animal Feed business is a leading compound feed player across the Cattle (Milk), Broiler, Layer, Fish and Shrimp Feed segments in India. Our feeds look after the health of the animals and ensure their yields are efficient.We are also the most significant Indian producers of Crude Palm Oil and Palm Kernel Oil through our Oil Palm Business.When it comes to our Food & Beverage brands, we produce a mix of Dairy, Poultry and Vending Foods through our businesses.

Such as our Creamline Dairy business, a leading player in milk and milk-based products in South India with our Jersey brand. Such as Godrej Tyson Foods, our joint venture with Tyson Foods, U.S.A, that manufactures and markets processed poultry and vegetarian products under brands like Real Good Chicken and Yummiez.We also create a range of beverage mixes such as tea, coffee, whitener, soups, special Indian drinks, range of tea bags and cold beverages, to ensure India enjoys its beverage breaks.Today, we have created a streamlined food supply chain that benefits both farmers as well as end consumers. Because, at Godrej, we believe that that's the only way to do it.
